About the job
Company Details
Channel Factory is a global advertising technology company focused on enabling brands and agencies to grow their video advertising impact across YouTube and social platforms. The company provides solutions that analyze video content at scale using advanced technology to ensure ads are placed in suitable, relevant, and performance-oriented environments.
Channel Factory’s platform emphasizes contextual alignment, allowing advertisers to connect their messages with the right content and audiences. By combining AI-powered content analysis, media optimization, and suitability controls, the company helps advertisers improve campaign efficiency, protect brand values, and drive stronger outcomes from video advertising.
The company supports both self-service and managed advertising approaches, enabling advertisers to either run campaigns independently or leverage Channel Factory’s expertise for planning, execution, and optimization. Its solutions are designed to deliver measurable results while maintaining responsible advertising standards across digital video ecosystems.
