Job Summary

We are looking for a Core Web Designer & WordPress Developer who can design, develop, and maintain the college’s website and online platforms. The ideal candidate should have expertise in WordPress, UI/UX design, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and website performance optimization. The role will focus on building responsive, SEO-friendly, and visually appealing web pages while ensuring smooth user experience and functionality.

Key Responsibilities

 Website Development & Maintenance
Design, develop, and maintain RCM’s official website and associated microsites.
Ensure mobile-friendly, responsive, and fast-loading web pages.
Optimize website structure and navigation for better user experience (UX).
Regularly update content, images, banners, and event pages.
Integrate third-party tools like CRM, payment gateways, and LMS.
 WordPress Development & Customization
Install, configure, and customize WordPress themes & plugins.
Develop custom WordPress themes, widgets, and shortcodes as per the institute’s branding.
Troubleshoot and resolve WordPress-related issues (performance, security, updates, etc.).
Implement best practices for WordPress security & backup strategies.
 HTML, CSS, JavaScript
Write clean, well-structured HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (jQuery).
Customize and develop interactive elements, forms, and animations.
Optimize website loading speed, SEO, and Core Web Vitals performance.
Fix cross-browser compatibility issues and conduct debugging.
 Website Performance Optimization
Optimize images, scripts, and database for faster page load speeds.
Monitor Google Page Speed Insights, Lighthouse, and GT metrix scores and make necessary improvements.
Ensure website is ADA/WCAG compliant for accessibility.
 Website Security & Data Protection
Implement SSL certificates, security plugins, and firewall protection.
Regularly update and maintain WordPress core, themes, and plugins.
Protect against hacks, malware, and DDoS attacks.
Ensure regular data backup strategies.
 API Integration & Third-Party Tools
Integrate CRM (HubSpot, Zoho), Payment Gateway (Razor pay, PayPal), and LMS.
Work with APIs for lead management, admissions, and online inquiry forms.
Ensure smooth connection with Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, and Tag Manager.
 Content Management & Collaboration
Work with digital marketing and admissions teams to update website content.
Ensure smooth content upload and integration for blog articles, event updates, and press releases.
Optimize landing pages for higher conversions and lead generation.

About The Role – Web Developer At RCM

The Regional College of Management, Bhubaneswar (RCM) is a premier management institution established in 1982. Recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and affiliated with AICTE, RCM has been a leader in management education in Eastern India. The institution is globally acknowledged for its academic excellence and is government recognized under Section 2(f) and 12(b) of the UGC Act.

As part of our digital expansion, we are seeking a skilled Core Web Designer & WordPress Developer to enhance our website, online portals, and digital assets to provide an engaging experience for students, faculty, and stakeholders.
